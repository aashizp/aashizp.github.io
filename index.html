about Ashim KC

Ashim KC is a well-known Nepali RnB/Pop Singer, Composer as well as Song-Writer hailing from town of Bardaghat. He has written and composed songs like "Saathi Veteu" and "Dekhawati Maya". Both of the songs are decorated by his own voice and have gained significant popularity all over Nepal. Additionally, He has also sung songs like "Sakdina" and "Aadha Raat". His works like "I am Sorry", "Timrai Mayama", "Ukali Orali", "Aayenau", "Timro Samjhana" and more which are sheduled to be released in 2016. You can find him at different social networks.
